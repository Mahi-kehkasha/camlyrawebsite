About our founder

Dr. Safina Naaz

I am a distinguished professional with a multifaceted background in psychology, education, and entrepreneurship. Driven by a passion for understanding human behaviour and guided by a resilient spirit, my journey has been marked by academic excellence, entrepreneurial endeavours, and a commitment to empowering individuals at various stages of life.

Early life and inspiration

My early experiences were shaped by my father's entrepreneurial pursuits and sparked my fascination for human behavior. The unwavering support of my dear husband and mother became a guiding force, influencing my journey across 26+ countries for business exploration at an early age. These experiences laid the foundation for my holistic approach to psychology and entrepreneurship.

The pursuit of education

My commitment to learning led me to achieve a Master of Arts in Psychology with a focus on Operations Research and Entrepreneurship. My academic journey culminated in a Doctorate in Philosophy with a specialization in Clinical Psychology. Specializing in Child Psychology and Counseling, I make sure to stay at the forefront of developments in the field, ensuring a comprehensive approach to personal growth.

Entrepreneurial beginnings

Combining an MBA in Operations Research and Entrepreneurship with a doctorate in psychology, I have built bridges between business rigour and compassionate care. Through train-the-trainer programmes and strategic initiatives, I remain committed to merging entrepreneurship with psychology so that more people can access quality mental wellness support.

Embracing life's ups and downs

Resilience has been central to my path. I integrate the lessons of psychology into my own growth and into every counselling and coaching relationship—helping clients navigate change, setback, and renewal with clarity and courage.

Helping clients achieve their goals

Whether as life counsellor, coach, or psychologist, I focus on empowering clients through collaborative planning, evidence-informed approaches, and steady encouragement—so you can move toward the outcomes that matter most to you.

Healing minds, transforming lives.

Assessment Tests

📋

There are numerous psychological tests used for various purposes in the field of psychology. Here’s a list of some common types:

Intelligence Tests

These tests measure intellectual abilities.

  • Stanford-Binet Intelligence Scales
  • Wechsler Adult Intelligence Scale (WAIS) 4 complete kit
  • Raven’s Progressive Matrices 2

Personality Tests

These assess different aspects of a person’s personality.

  • Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Inventory (MMPI) hands free kit
  • Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)
  • Big Five Personality Traits

Behavioural Assessments

Observe, describe, and measure behaviour.

  • Functional Behaviour Assessment (FBA)
  • ABC (Antecedent, Behaviour, Consequence) Chart

Developmental and Educational Assessments

Assess developmental stages and educational needs.

  • Denver Developmental Screening Test (DDST)
  • Dermatoglyphic multiple intelligence test

Mood and Anxiety Tests

Assess the presence and severity of mood and anxiety disorders.

  • Beck Depression Inventory (BDI)
  • Hamilton Anxiety Rating Scale (HAM-A)

Substance Use and Addiction Assessments

Evaluate the extent of substance use and addiction.

  • Alcohol Use Disorders Identification Test
  • Drug Abuse Screening Test (DAST)

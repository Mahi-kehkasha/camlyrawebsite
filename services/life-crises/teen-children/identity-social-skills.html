Identity & social skills

Teen years are a time of exploration and change. Therapy helps teens strengthen self-esteem, clarify values, and build communication skills that support healthier friendships and boundaries.

Importance

  • Self-esteem: Reduce self-doubt and build confidence.
  • Belonging: Improve connection and friendship skills.
  • Boundaries: Learn to say no and handle pressure.
  • Communication: Express needs clearly and respectfully.
  • Resilience: Cope with rejection and conflict.

Why It Is Provided

  • Social stress: Anxiety in groups or new settings.
  • Isolation: Difficulty connecting with peers.
  • Pressure: Comparison, social media, and fitting in.
  • Identity: Exploring values and self-concept.
  • Support: A safe space to build confidence.

What We Work On

  • Skills: Conversation, assertiveness, and repair.
  • Mindset: Reduce negative self-talk and shame.
  • Boundaries: Healthy limits and decision-making.
  • Values: Clarify identity and priorities.
  • Confidence: Practice exposure and resilience tools.

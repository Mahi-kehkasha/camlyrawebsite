Family sessions for teens

Family sessions help teens and caregivers understand each other better, reduce conflict, and create healthier routines. These sessions focus on communication patterns, boundaries, and collaborative problem-solving.

Importance

  • Connection: Rebuild trust and emotional safety.
  • Communication: Learn healthier ways to talk and listen.
  • Boundaries: Create clarity around expectations and rules.
  • Support: Reduce stress for teens and caregivers.
  • Teamwork: Strengthen shared problem-solving at home.

Why It Is Provided

  • Conflict: Frequent fights or shutdowns.
  • Transitions: School pressure, identity changes, life events.
  • Behaviour: Tension around routines and responsibilities.
  • Emotions: Stress, anxiety, or mood challenges at home.
  • Guidance: Consistent strategies for caregivers.

What We Work On

  • Patterns: Identify triggers and escalation cycles.
  • Skills: Repair after conflict and reduce reactivity.
  • Agreements: Set boundaries and shared expectations.
  • Support: Improve empathy and validation.
  • Routine: Build structure that reduces stress.
